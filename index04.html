A totally different sports car

Different by design, different by philosophy

Lee Noble's finest car

Designed from a clean sheet by world renowned automotive skill Lee Noble the car is a significant step up in performance, capability and ethos from any of his previous works.

The finest handling car in the world is now also one of the quickest cars available on sale today.

A serious track day car

This will keep up with everything. Superlative handling personally signed off by Lee Noble, 500bhp from a mid mounted engine, 0.60 in just over 2.5 seconds, track day tyres, with 'cut slicks' an option if you want them, full FIA specification roll cage and 5 point harness for safety, and enough room to store your helmet and gear and still take along a passenger with you.

Mind bending performance

By utlising a new V6 power plant from General Motors the car offers 500bhp and 690lbft, which when coupled with just 950kg wet ensurs that the performance is something that you have never experienced before. But it's not all power, full race orientated suspension all round with inboard damping at the rear ensures that the legendary Lee Noble handling is taken to even higher levels.

A practical road car

Due to our unique double clam roof and windows, entry and exit from the car is easy when compared to cars like Caterhams, Radicals, Lotus, Nobles to name just a few, and yet we also have a sizeable boot enabling you to have enough two full adults in the car and yet take your shopping home from the supermarket, have a long weekend tour of the country side.

True innovation

Our unique system means you can remove the roof and glass side windows and leave them track side, saving weight and yet ensuring that you can feel the wind in your hair and experience the full sound of our sports orientated exhaust and induction system. Never before have you had so much fun, and gone so quick, around a track.

The Car

Fundamentally different, fundamentally better

Engine

V6. Twin Turbo. Inbuilt water charge cooler. 500bhp. 690lbft. 2015 design, Euro6 emissions capable. Enough said.

Gearbox

Mounted as a structural element the 6 speed manual is rated for 800bhp. Tacticle, lightning fast, super strong.

Chassis

Hybrid aluminimum honeycomb tub with steel spaceframe front and rear. Incredible strength, incredible protection, incredible handling.

Suspension

Highly compact twin wishbone coil-over-damper front suspension for excellent packaging. Inboard push-rod rear for optimum balance.

Bodywork

Full carbon-fibre for incredible strengh yet incredibly lightweight. Entire body weight is only 85kg.

Comfort

Sculpted carbon tub seats giving excellent support. Adjustable seats and steering means anyone can be accomodated.

Practicality

Without any doors but easier to get in and out from than a Lotus Elise and a full size boot enable to take a weekends luggage.
